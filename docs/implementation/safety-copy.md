# Safety Copy — DivePrep Buddy

Required for MVP. Review with Security & Safety agent before public deploy.

## Primary disclaimer (first launch)

```text
DivePrep Buddy helps you organize pre-dive preparation.

It does not replace dive training, professional instruction,
certified dive safety procedures, or a dive computer.

Always verify your equipment physically and follow your training.
```

**Accept button:** `I understand`

## Footer (checklist + buddy-check screens)

```text
Preparation assistant only — not a safety authority.
```

## Ready summary (allowed)

```text
Preparation reviewed.
Remember to verify all equipment physically before entering the water.
```

## Forbidden phrases (never in UI)

- `You are safe to dive`
- `Guaranteed safe`
- `Certified ready`
- `NDL` / decompression advice
- Gas mix recommendations
- Medical clearance language

## Preferred verbs

verify, check, review, remember, confirm, prepare

## Buddy-check tone

Each step is a **reminder to verify physically**, not an automated pass/fail.

Example:

- OK: `Verify BCD inflates and deflates correctly`
- BAD: `BCD is safe`

## Offline copy

```text
Offline mode active. Your checklists still work on this device.
```

## Legal positioning

App assists with **organization and memory**, not dive planning authority.

Log any copy change that touches safety wording in `agent-runs/`.
