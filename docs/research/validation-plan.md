# DivePrep Buddy — Validation Plan

## Goal

Validate whether divers feel enough preparation stress and checklist friction to repeatedly use the product.

---

# Key Questions

- Do divers already use preparation checklists?
- What gets forgotten most often?
- Is buddy-check friction common?
- Is travel preparation painful?

---

# Validation Steps

## Step 1 — Diver Interviews

Interview:

- recreational divers;
- destination divers;
- dive instructors.

---

## Step 2 — Concierge Checklist Test

Manually generate contextual checklists.

Observe whether divers use them before dives.

---

## Step 3 — Prototype Validation

Test:

- checklist UX;
- buddy-check flow;
- travel prep workflow.

---

# Strong Signal

```text
Divers repeatedly open the app before dives.
```
